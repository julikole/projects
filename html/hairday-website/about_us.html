WHO WE ARE

Pria, the owner of Hair Day, earned her certificate as a colorist and immediately started specializing in edgy, custom coloring techniques.

She opened the salon with her good friend and talented hairdresser, Garry, and together they created a "treat yourself" environment for the clients.

Today the staff of Hair Day consists of seven talented professionals: two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist and one make-up artist.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Based in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color and styling, in addition to makeup and nail services. We proud ourselves on our warm and relaxing atmosphere, and offer edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts.

The salon can only accommodate a small number of coloring appointments each week, so we kindly ask our clients to book well in advance. Please refer to the main page for booking.

And don't forget to check out the latest makeup and nail tutorials on our website for inspiration!
